Question 3: Travel Discovery App

Create a travel discovery app using Ionic-React with the following structure:

1. Welcome Screen:

  - Prompt users to add a travel card with a stock image and some text to the welcome screen.
  - Include an icon to indicate the travel theme.
  - Provide a "Get Started" button to navigate to the travel destinations screen.

2. Travel Destinations:

  - On tapping "Get Started" on the Welcome Screen, users should be directed to the Travel Destinations screen.
  - Display a list of travel destination cards with stock images and brief descriptions.
  - Include icons representing different travel destinations.


3. Travel Tips:

  - Users can navigate to the Travel Tips screen from the Travel Destinations screen.
  - Display travel tips content with relevant icons to enhance the user experience.
